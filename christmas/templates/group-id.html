Training


For this project we needed to collect and understand our data before we could make future predictions of hurricanes from the Atlantic coast. We collected our data from https://www.kaggle.com/noaa/hurricane-database/data Next we need to begin understanding our data so we could pick out a model. We chose to use an autoregression model because we mainly wanted to use our data to predict future observations. To make sure we fitting our data fairly, we split our data into thirds and used the first two thirds to train on while leaving the last third to test aganist. Below is the results of the test with the final Mean Squared Error of the predictions being 12.448.
